Apr 8

Serta mulia,

It's your day papa. Funny to think until this sentence i already don't know what to say lol. I just wanna say thank you. Thank you for being my father. I'm really glad that you're my father, and i'm your daughter. Idk... r u still excited with ur birthday or nah. Cuz as far as i know, adults tend to treat their birthday casually, just like the other days. Maybe busy with work or some adult kind of thing. Buttt if papa doesn't take this day beautifully, like not romanticize the day you were born, it's ok. Let me take that. With this awkward sentences that maybe you're ain't reading this message.
Suddenly it's April, suddenly it's spring again, & suddenly it's 8th of April, your birthday. Just a usual day like any other day. You go to work, driving me to the place where i'll take the school bus. And i pray. I pray, pray,

for the sake of God

takut. tbh i'm scared of everything that could've done. i hope papa still young, i wish i'm still a kid. kenapa orang tua ngga bisa hidup selamanya aja sih? jujur idk what i'm gonna to do if papa & mama are no longer in this world. like, can i...? takut ngga bisa ngasih yang terbaik setelah semua yang mama papa lakuin untuk kita semua. are we already have been a good kid, a good daughter to you? i'm sorry papa. i'm sorry if it looks like i always quiet. well, i just don't know how to express my feelings genuinely. it's like my words are choking me to death, stuck in my throat & never i dare to say it loudly. or i'm just gonna burst out of tears, like rn. i have no idea since when i'm become soo emotional. hal se basic nulis ini aja udah berat bgt, bikin nangis. tapi ngga tau knp kaya gini. kayak... ngga tau aja, maaf kalau jarang ngomomg sama mama papa, tp setelah dipikir-pikir kana ngga mau ngomong karena malu? malu karena sampai skrg blm tau mau apa, bingung. padahal bentar lagi yakan, tapi kek ngerasa ngga punya pilihan. udah di detik detik takut ngga jadi apa apa. zaman sekarang makin mudah tapi makin susah juga :]. maaff kalau jadi curhatt i just want you to know what ur second daughter felt. bsc i can't speaking it frankly, directly to you. jadi papa... sekali lagi selamat ulang tahun. semoga segala hal tersemogakan. semoga papa sehat selalu.
